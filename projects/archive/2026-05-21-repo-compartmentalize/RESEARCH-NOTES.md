## Top-level `bin/` (root shims) vs `plugins/<name>/bin/`
- Root `bin/loom` (16 lines), `bin/guild`, `bin/griot` — minimal, no Node-version check, no symlink-safe resolution. Comment claims "Generated by install.sh" (file deleted; see `cli/no-install-sh-refs.test.ts`).
- Plugin `plugins/*/bin/<cli>` (49 lines) — fuller: symlink-safe + Node-version-check + ENTRY existence.
